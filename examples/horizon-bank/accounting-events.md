# Horizon Bank Accounting-Event Catalogue

This catalogue is an author model of business occurrences that can create, reverse or adjust an accounting consequence for the fictional Horizon Bank. It identifies the event and the accountable finance responsibility without inventing a chart of accounts, posting amount, tax treatment or accounting-policy conclusion.

`Accounting Event Class` is a local grouping for traceability. Detailed debit, credit, subledger, currency, valuation, materiality and reversal rules remain governed design work.

## Governance rules

- An accounting event describes a business occurrence, not a complete journal template.
- Detailed posting rules must state legal entity, product, currency, accounting basis, effective date, source event, reversal behaviour and responsible subledger.
- Product applications originate business facts. The product subledger and general ledger consume governed accounting events under controlled mappings; they do not redefine the originating business lifecycle.
- Reconciliation identifies differences but does not itself authorise a journal, customer adjustment or external resubmission.
