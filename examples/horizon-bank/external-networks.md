# Horizon Bank External-Network Catalogue

This catalogue identifies logical external connectivity that a full-service bank may need. It deliberately avoids naming providers, asserting memberships, claiming certification or selecting a jurisdiction-specific network. A real implementation must replace each assumption with verified legal-entity, market, jurisdiction, contract and operational evidence.

## Governance rules

- Every network record is a logical placeholder until applicability, provider, legal-entity participation and jurisdiction are verified.
- An external-network record is not evidence of membership, sponsorship, certification, direct technical connectivity or contractual entitlement.
- Network exchanges still require governed interface contracts, security controls, operational ownership, reconciliation and recovery design.
