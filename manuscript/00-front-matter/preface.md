# Preface

## Purpose of the preface

Explain why architecture modelling often appears harder than it is, why learners become confused by overlapping notations, and how this book uses a question-first approach.

## Planned themes

- The problem of diagram collections without guidance
- Why no single notation is sufficient
- The difference between learning symbols and learning architectural thinking
- The value of a simple example followed by a full banking case study
- How the reader should practise while reading

> Draft after the prototype chapter establishes the final voice.
