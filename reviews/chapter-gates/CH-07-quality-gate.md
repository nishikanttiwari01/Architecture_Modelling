## Review perspectives

### Beginner reader

The chapter explains ArchiMate as a way to connect architecture domains. It avoids a specification-first presentation and uses concrete Horizon Bank examples.

### Enterprise architect

The chapter covers the main viewpoints a beginner needs: capability, layered, application cooperation, technology, motivation and migration. It stresses relationship meaning and traceability across business, application and technology layers.

### Solution architect

The C4 and BPMN comparisons clarify when ArchiMate is the wrong first notation. This reduces the risk of using ArchiMate for detailed process flow or software internals.

### Diagram reviewer

Six figure specifications exist and are registered. Source files and exports must wait for author approval of the specifications under DEC-007 and the repository diagram workflow.

### Source and copyright checker

The chapter uses `[OPEN-GROUP-ARCHIMATE-3.2]` for formal terminology and paraphrases the language concepts. It does not reproduce official diagrams, tables or long specification text.
