## Relevant summary

The chapter explains selecting user-relevant indicators, defining objectives and using measurement windows. It cautions against treating every available metric as a service indicator.

## Terminology and version notes

Define SLI and SLO at first use. Do not present an objective as a guarantee or universal target.
