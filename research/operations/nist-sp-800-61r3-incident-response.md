## Relevant summary

Revision 3 frames incident response as part of cybersecurity risk management and uses Cybersecurity Framework 2.0 functions to connect preparation, detection, response, recovery and improvement.

## Terminology and version notes

Do not substitute this high-level lifecycle for an organisation's detailed incident procedure.
