## Relevant summary

The OMG UML 2.5.1 specification is the official source for UML terminology, abstract syntax, semantics and diagram categories. For this beginner handbook, it is used to verify the names and broad purposes of UML diagram types. The chapter paraphrases the relevant concepts and avoids reproducing specification text, metamodel diagrams or tables.

## Terminology and version notes

UML 2.5.1 is the formal version used for Chapter 4 and deployment terminology reused in Chapter 11. The manuscript explains a practical subset for architecture work rather than the full UML language. Interaction diagrams are treated as part of UML behaviour modelling, with sequence diagrams used as the main beginner example.
