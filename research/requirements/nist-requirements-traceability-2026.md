## Notes

NIST defines traceability as a discernible association among logical entities, including requirements, system elements and verification. Its requirements-verification page lists completeness, consistency, correctness, modifiability, ranking, traceability, unambiguity, understandability and verifiability as analysis concerns. Chapter 25 paraphrases these points.
