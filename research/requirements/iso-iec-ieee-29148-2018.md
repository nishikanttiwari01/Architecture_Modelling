# ISO/IEC/IEEE 29148:2018 source note

- **Source ID:** ISO-IEC-IEEE-29148-2018
- **Organisation:** ISO, IEC and IEEE
- **Title:** Systems and software engineering, Life cycle processes, Requirements engineering
- **Version:** Edition 2, 2018
- **Status checked:** Confirmed in 2024; revision project recorded
- **URL:** https://www.iso.org/standard/72089.html
- **Accessed:** 2026-07-11
- **Use:** Requirements-engineering life-cycle scope and information-item context for Chapter 25.

## Notes

The official abstract says the standard specifies requirements-engineering processes and related information items for systems, software, products and services across the life cycle. Chapter 25 paraphrases this scope. It does not reproduce normative wording or claim conformance.
