## Relevant summary

The ArchiMate 3.2 specification defines a visual modelling language for describing enterprise architecture. It organises architecture content across business, application and technology concerns, with strategy, motivation and implementation and migration concepts for describing intent and change. The language is intended to support integrated architecture descriptions rather than replace specialised process, software or infrastructure notations.

For this handbook, the source supports the official vocabulary and the existence of the major concept groups. The chapter paraphrases the concepts and uses original examples based on the Simple Online Store and Horizon Bank case studies.
