## Relevant summary

Official tool sources support a practical, neutral comparison for Chapter 7:

- Archi is useful as a free desktop ArchiMate modelling tool and model repository for learning and small teams, but its public download page states Archi 5.9.0 supports ArchiMate 3.2.
- ADOIT Community Edition, Visual Paradigm Online Free, diagrams.net and PlantUML provide free or low-cost entry points with different trade-offs between repository semantics, drawing convenience and reproducibility.
- Visual Paradigm Enterprise, Bizzdesign Horizzon, Sparx Enterprise Architect, Avolution ABACUS, ADOIT, OrbusInfinity and MEGA HOPEX are paid enterprise modelling or architecture-management tools. Their suitability depends on repository governance, collaboration, analysis, integration and organisational standards.
- diagrams.net is a general drawing tool, not a governed ArchiMate repository.
- PlantUML can create reproducible teaching views, but it is not a full semantic enterprise architecture repository.
