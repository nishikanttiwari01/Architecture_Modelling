## Relevant summary

BIAN describes the Service Landscape as one possible organisation of a standard set of Service Domains. Its release page identifies 14.0 as the February 2026 release. Because names and operations changed, every exact BIAN object used in the book must record version 14.0 and be checked against the official artefact rather than copied from an older example.

The release page does not state that Service Domains are deployable services, applications, teams or microservices. Horizon Bank mappings therefore remain candidate responsibility mappings until separately justified.

## Terminology and version notes
