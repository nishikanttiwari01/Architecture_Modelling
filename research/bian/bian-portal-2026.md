## Relevant summary

The official portal page identifies tools for exploring Service Domains and Business Scenarios. It supports using BIAN to inform candidate logical responsibilities and interactions. It does not state that a Service Domain is a bank process step, application or deployable microservice, and it does not replace organisation-specific process ownership, controls or exceptions.
