## Relevant summary

The Service Landscape page describes a reference organisation of Service Domains and records release 14.0 as the February 2026 release. Its release notes say that definitions were rationalised and that some payment Service Domains changed, so exact mappings must be checked against the versioned artefact.

The Practitioner Guide describes Service Domains, Business Scenarios, wireframes, Service Operations and Semantic APIs as related design artefacts. It also describes scenarios and wireframes as examples that can be adapted without changing the purpose of the participating Service Domains. The guide discusses mapping conceptual functional designs to application modules, but it does not remove the need to decide application, software-service, deployment, data or team boundaries.
