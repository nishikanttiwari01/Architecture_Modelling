## Relevant summary

The 2025 principles organise sound credit-risk management around four connected areas: a suitable credit-risk environment, a sound credit-granting process, appropriate credit administration and monitoring, and adequate controls. This supports modelling lending as a lifecycle rather than treating a decision engine or origination workflow as the whole domain. The principles are directed to banks and supervisors, but their application still depends on jurisdiction, portfolio and institutional context.

## Terminology and version notes

Use `credit granting`, `credit administration`, `measurement`, `monitoring` and `control` as related responsibilities, not interchangeable stages or application names.
