## Relevant summary

The FATF Recommendations provide a risk-based international baseline for anti-money laundering, counter-terrorist financing and counter-proliferation financing controls. The customer due-diligence model covers more than a one-time identity check. It includes customer and beneficial-owner identification and verification, understanding the relationship, and ongoing review. Local law, regulation and supervisory interpretation determine the exact evidence, thresholds, timing and reporting duties that apply to a bank.

## Terminology and version notes

Use `customer due diligence` as the full term at first use and `CDD` thereafter. Know Your Customer (KYC) is common industry language, but the chapter uses CDD when referring specifically to the FATF standard.
