## Relevant summary

The report defines and analyses correspondent-banking arrangements, including traditional and nested relationships, and discusses payment messages, know-your-customer information and legal-entity identifiers. This supports modelling the correspondent relationship separately from the individual payment instruction and from the internal nostro position. The report does not prescribe Horizon Bank's application decomposition.

## Terminology and version notes

Use `correspondent bank` for the institution providing account and payment services to a respondent bank. Use `nostro` only after explaining that it is the bank's view of its account held with another institution.
