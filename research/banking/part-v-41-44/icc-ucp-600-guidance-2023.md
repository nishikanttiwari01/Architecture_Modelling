## Relevant summary

The ICC guidance addresses documentary-credit practice under UCP 600, including transport-document notation, compliance of presentations and notices of refusal. It supports retaining document examination, discrepancy evidence and authorised disposition as explicit responsibilities. The chapter does not reproduce the rules or claim that UCP 600 applies to every trade-finance instrument.

## Terminology and version notes

Use `Uniform Customs and Practice for Documentary Credits (UCP 600)` at first use. State that applicability depends on the instrument terms and applicable law.
