## Relevant summary

The Committee on Payments and Market Infrastructures provides a common vocabulary for payment, clearing, settlement and financial-market-infrastructure concepts. The terms support separate architecture responsibilities for accepting an instruction, exchanging and calculating obligations, and completing the transfer of settlement assets. They also support separate treatment of foreign-exchange and securities settlement mechanisms.

## Terminology and version notes

Define `real-time gross settlement`, `deferred net settlement`, `payment versus payment` and `delivery versus payment` at first use. Do not infer that any one mechanism applies to Horizon Bank without a verified network and jurisdiction decision.
