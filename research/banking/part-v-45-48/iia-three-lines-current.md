## Relevant summary

The current Institute of Internal Auditors statement describes a Three Lines Model and emphasises accountability and the distinct contribution of organisational roles. It identifies internal audit as the independent third line providing objective assurance. Architecture should allow coordination and shared identifiers without granting operational management the ability to alter independent audit conclusions or evidence.
