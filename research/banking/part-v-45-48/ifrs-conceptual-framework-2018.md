## Relevant summary

The framework explains the objective and qualitative characteristics of useful financial reporting, the reporting entity, the elements of financial statements, recognition and derecognition, measurement, presentation and disclosure. For architecture, it supports separating a business occurrence from the governed accounting interpretation and from later report presentation. It does not prescribe Horizon Bank's event catalogue, chart of accounts or posting implementation.

## Terminology and version notes

The revised framework was issued in March 2018. The IFRS Foundation states that it is not a Standard. Applicable IFRS Accounting Standards and local requirements take precedence for actual accounting policies.
