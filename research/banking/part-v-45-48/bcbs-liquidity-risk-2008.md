## Supported claims

- Normative or interpretive: Official principles; the Horizon Bank operating and application model is an author interpretation.

## Relevant summary

The principles treat liquidity as the ability to fund asset growth and meet obligations when due without unacceptable loss. They stress governance, a bank-wide risk tolerance, measurement of contractual and contingent cash flows, severe stress testing, contingency funding planning, intraday liquidity and collateral management. The book uses these concerns to test whether treasury models join decisions to positions, assumptions, limits, evidence and executable actions.

## Terminology and version notes
