## Relevant summary

Revision 4 covers identity proofing and enrolment, authenticator management, authentication protocols, federation and assertions. It also integrates security, privacy and customer-experience considerations. The chapter uses this separation to show why a channel, a customer identity service and a customer entitlement service have different responsibilities.

## Terminology and version notes

Revision 4 supersedes NIST SP 800-63-3. The suite includes separate A, B and C volumes. A real bank must choose assurance and control requirements from its own risk and legal context.
