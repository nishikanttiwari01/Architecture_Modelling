## Relevant summary

The Basel Committee revised its operational-risk principles in 2021, including stronger treatment of change management and information and communication technology. This supports treating modernisation as governed operational change rather than as a purely technical replacement. It does not prescribe Horizon Bank's migration waves, catalogue fields or gate names.
