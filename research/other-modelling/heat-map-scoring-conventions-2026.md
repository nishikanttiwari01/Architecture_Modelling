## Relevant summary

The Chapter 13 heat-map example is an illustrative teaching view. It should not imply that red, amber and green have universal meanings. The figure specification requires visible `H`, `M` and `L` markers, separate dimensions, criteria notes, date, owner, version and an explicit statement that the scores are illustrative.
