## Relevant summary

The public G211 publication page identifies the business-capabilities guide as a TOGAF Series Guide from The Open Group Architecture Forum Business Architecture Work Stream. The page says the guide addresses creating a capability map, aligning it with other business architecture viewpoints and using business capabilities to enhance business analysis and planning.

The public G178 publication page identifies the value-stream guide as a TOGAF Series Guide from the same work stream. The page says value streams are core business architecture elements and that the guide addresses identifying, defining, modelling and mapping value streams to other key business architecture components.

Chapter 13 should use these sources only for public high-level source support. It should not reproduce guide content, examples, diagrams or restricted text. The chapter's Horizon Bank examples are original teaching examples.
