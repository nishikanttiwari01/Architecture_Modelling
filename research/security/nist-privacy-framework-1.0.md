- Normative or interpretive: Interpretive modelling guidance based on official privacy-risk framing.

## Relevant summary

NIST Privacy Framework 1.0 provides a risk-management framing for privacy. Chapter 12 uses it to explain why data classification is useful but incomplete: privacy review also needs to consider why data is processed, who can see it, where it flows, how long it is kept and whether unnecessary data can be minimised.
