## Relevant summary

Schneier's article explains attack trees as a way to model a security goal from an attacker's perspective. The harmful goal appears at the root, and branches show possible ways to achieve it. Chapter 12 uses this source to support the basic root, branch, AND and OR explanation without copying examples.
