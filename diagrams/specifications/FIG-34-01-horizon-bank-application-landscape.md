# FIG-34-01: Horizon Bank application and integration landscape

## Purpose

Show the complete application taxonomy, cross-cutting security and external ecosystems.

## Audience

Enterprise, solution, application, integration, security and operations architects.

## Question answered

Which application families support the bank and how do they exchange information?

## Notation

Draw.io layered application landscape after author approval.

## Required elements

The eleven application layers defined in Chapter 34, lifecycle markers, legal-entity variants and external networks.

## Required relationships

Label API, event, batch/file, workflow and external-network interactions at overview level.

## Main flow or structure

Channels at the top, product and control responsibilities in the middle, finance/data/technology below, external ecosystem at the boundary.

## Alternative and exception flows

Show repair/workflow path and transitional integration.

## Scope

Enterprise application families, not product-level inventory.

## Exclusions

Unapproved vendor names, detailed interfaces and deployment nodes.

## Accessibility requirements

Lifecycle uses text and border styles; connections remain readable at a two-page spread.
